Enclosed cat litter boxes help contain odor, reduce tracking, and give your cat a sense of privacy. Whether you’re battling a messy kicker, a picky feline, or a tight budget, a covered box can make a real difference in your home. Choosing the right style, from top-entry to dome to hooded, depends on your cat’s habits and your space, but the best designs balance easy cleaning with solid odor control. When shopping, consider the size of the entry, the height of the walls, the quality of the latch or seal, and how easy it is to scoop. Self-cleaning options add convenience but cost more; simple hooded boxes are affordable but may need more frequent cleaning. Our verdict

Choosing the right enclosed litter box comes down to your cat’s preferences, your space, and your budget.
